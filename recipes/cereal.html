Cereal

Delicious and Juicy

This gourmet breakfast dish is a delicacy in eastern Burbank. Although the ingredients are hard to find (due to the impact of climate change on local cereal plants), if you find yourself a reliable and affordable cereal dealer this recipe is a MUST TRY. You know what they say.. "When in Burbank, do as the Burbankians do."

Ingredients

1/2 Serving

  • 1 cup milk (or milk alternative)
  • 1 cup cereal, diced

Steps

  1. Mix all of the ingredients in a large bowl with bite size spoon until evenly distributed.
  2. Enjoy!
