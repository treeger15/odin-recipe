Breakfast Sandwich

Easy and Tasty

This simple yet timeless breakfast sandwich is my favorite saturday morning go-to breakfast dish. I prefer a simple egg and cheese english muffin sandwich with horsey sauce and green onions. But there are so many variations and you really can't go wrong.

Ingredients

1 Serving

  • 1 English Muffin
  • 1-2 Eggs (or Eqq White equivalent)
  • 1-2 Meat slices of your choice (optional)
  • 1/3 Cup of shredded cheese
  • 1 tsp green onion, finely chopped
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil (or butter)
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)
  • Horseradish Sauce (to taste-optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the english muffin in half.
  2. Heat a large non-stick pan over medium heat and drizzle with 1 Tbsp oil.
  3. Place the english muffon on one side of the pan cut-side down. Crack the eggs (side by side, if using 2) on the other half of the pan and break the yolks with a spatula.
  4. When the eggs are almost fully cooked, season with salt and pepper as desired, add green onions and half of the shredded cheese.
  5. Place the english muffins cut-side-down on top of the eggs and lightly press down with the spatula.
  6. Flip the sammy over so that the egg side is up, add the meat, a bit of horsey sauce, then top with more cheese.
  7. Fold the sandwhich together and remove from heat.
  8. Enjoy!
